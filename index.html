About the Workshop

AWOC-JKWOC 2025 is an international workshop designed to share the latest trends and technologies in ocean color research and strengthen networking among researchers in the Asian region.

Hosted by the Korea Institute of Ocean Science & Technology (KIOST), this workshop will bring together experts from various fields including satellite remote sensing, marine biology, marine chemistry, and marine physics to discuss the future of ocean color research.

Abstract Submission

Submission Guidelines

Important Dates

  • Abstract Submission Deadline: October 15, 2025
  • Notification of Acceptance: October 30, 2025
  • Final Program Release: November 5, 2025

Format Requirements

  • Length: Maximum 500 words
  • Language: English
  • Format: Plain text or Word document
  • Font: Times New Roman, 12pt
  • Figures: Maximum 2 figures/tables allowed

Abstract Structure

  • Title: Clear and concise (maximum 20 words)
  • Authors: Full names and affiliations
  • Introduction: Background and objectives
  • Methods: Brief methodology description
  • Results: Key findings
  • Conclusions: Main conclusions and implications

Research Topics

  • Ocean color remote sensing algorithms
  • Phytoplankton ecology and biogeochemistry
  • Coastal and inland water studies
  • AI and machine learning applications
  • Satellite data validation and calibration
  • Climate change and ocean color
  • Hyperspectral ocean color research
